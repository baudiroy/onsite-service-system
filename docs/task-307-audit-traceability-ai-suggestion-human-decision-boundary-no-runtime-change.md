# Task 307 - Audit Traceability For AI Suggestion And Human Decision Boundary / No Runtime Change

## Scope And Non-goals

This document continues the Audit Log / Evidence Traceability branch after Task303 through Task306.

Task307 defines future-only audit traceability boundaries for AI suggestions, AI risk flags, retrieved source references, confidence categories, explanations, human accept / reject / edit decisions, official write proposals, and official record writes.

The goal is to preserve AI-ready capability while preventing AI suggestions, confidence scores, retrieved sources, or audits from becoming official business decisions by themselves.

## Why AI Suggestion / Human Decision Audit Traceability Is Needed After Task306

Task306 defined audit traceability for data access, reports, exports, downloads, scheduled reports, customer self-service lookup, AI retrieval, and RAG retrieval.

The next risk is treating AI output as if it were a human decision or deterministic business rule.

AI can assist completion summaries, appointment outcome classification, customer-visible wording, billing/settlement checks, quality risk flags, safe-deny wording, and report explanations. But AI suggestions must remain separate from official records until a permitted human or deterministic workflow accepts, edits, validates, and writes the official data.

Task307 defines this boundary before any AI/RAG, audit, official write, Data Access, API, Admin, DB, or runtime work is approved.

## Definitions

### AI Suggestion Event

AI suggestion event is a future trace that an AI-generated suggestion, draft, classification, summary, or recommendation was produced.

It is not an official record.

### AI Risk Flag Event

AI risk flag event is a future trace that AI suggested a risk category, anomaly, missing-field reminder, SLA concern, quality concern, settlement concern, or complaint risk.

It is not a final decision.

### AI Retrieved Source Reference

AI retrieved source reference is a future masked pointer to a permission-approved source used by AI/RAG.

It is not a permission bypass and must not expose full source content in audit.

### AI Confidence Category

AI confidence category is a future coarse confidence label or range.

Confidence is not approval.

### AI Explanation Summary

AI explanation summary is a future minimized reason or explanation category.

It must be masked and must not contain raw sensitive payload.

### Human Accept Event

Human accept event records a future authorized human decision to accept an AI suggestion or proposal within a permitted workflow.

Acceptance may still require validation before official write.

### Human Reject Event

Human reject event records a future human decision not to use an AI suggestion.

Rejected suggestions must not update official records.

### Human Edit Event

Human edit event records a future human modification to AI output.

Audit should record edit category, not sensitive raw diff.

### Official Write Proposal

Official write proposal is a future candidate change generated by AI, system logic, or human workflow for possible official record update.

It is not an official write by itself.

### Official Record Write Event

Official record write event is a future trace of a validated, authorized, official data change performed by a permitted workflow.

It must remain separate from AI suggestion generation.

### AI Raw Sensitive Payload

AI raw sensitive payload is raw prompt, response, retrieved content, provider payload, or context containing sensitive or private data.

It must not be stored in audit logs.

### Audit-safe AI Metadata

Audit-safe AI metadata is minimized metadata about AI task category, source category, masked references, confidence category, human decision category, and usage category.

## Boundary Principles

- AI suggestion is not official record.
- AI suggestion audit does not mean AI can write official data.
- AI confidence is not approval.
- AI retrieved source is not permission bypass.
- Human accept does not mean every context can automatically write official record.
- Human edit must be traceable by edited value category, but audit must not store sensitive raw diff.
- AI cannot automatically approve quotes.
- AI cannot automatically approve settlement.
- AI cannot automatically dispatch.
- AI cannot automatically complete a service report.
- AI cannot automatically close complaint.
- AI cannot automatically consent to fees.
- AI cannot automatically change official Case status.
- AI cannot bypass Data Access Control.
- AI cannot bypass organization isolation.
- AI cannot bypass permission, entitlement, masking, or human approval workflow.
- Official write must use the correct business workflow, validation, permission, and deterministic rules.

## Human Decision Traceability Rules

Human accept / reject / edit must be future traceable by:

- actor category,
- actor organization scope,
- timestamp,
- task category,
- source suggestion category,
- decision category,
- masked target reference,
- permission category,
- validation category,
- correlation id.

Human edit must avoid storing sensitive raw diff in audit.

Different official record writes still require their own workflow, permission, and validation.

AI suggestion adoption must not bypass approval workflow.

AI suggestion adoption must not bypass customer fee consent, quote approval, settlement approval, complaint closure, notification consent, or official completion rules.

## Interaction With Previous Branches

### AI / RAG

AI/RAG must stay closed-domain, permission-aware, tenant-isolated, auditable, human-controlled, and RAG-grounded.

Retrieved sources must be masked references in audit, not full source payloads.

### Data Access Control

Data Access Control is required before AI retrieval and before audit access.

AI cannot query unfiltered database or vector data.

### Engineer Mobile AI-assisted Completion

Engineer Mobile AI can assist completion summaries, missing-field reminders, and classification.

Engineer or authorized human workflow must confirm official completion data.

### Billing / Settlement

AI may flag billing/settlement risk or suggest checks.

AI cannot approve settlement or official payable amounts.

### Operations / Quality

AI may flag complaint risk, quality risk, callback priority, or operations anomalies.

AI cannot close complaints or decide corrective action by itself.

### Customer Channel Identity

AI may suggest safe-deny wording or support summaries.

AI cannot approve binding, merge identities, decide verification conflicts, or send provider messages.

### Audit Evidence

AI suggestions may link to evidence references, but evidence and approval remain separate.

Human decision audit must be separated from AI suggestion audit.

### SaaS AI Add-on Usage Tracking

AI usage may link to usage tracking separately.

Usage tracking must not store raw prompts, raw responses, or sensitive retrieved content.
